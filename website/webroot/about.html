About AIQA

AIQA is a quality assurance platform designed specifically for AI systems. We make it easy to test, trace, and improve your AI applications.

The Challenge

Generative AI has exploded into mainstream business. ChatGPT, DALL-E, and similar systems are driving innovation across many sectors. But as businesses rely more on AI, quality assurance becomes critical.

Unfortunately, AI systems often receive less testing than traditional software, even though they need more. They're unpredictable by nature, which makes them harder to test. But that unpredictability is exactly why they need rigorous testing.

Why Traditional Testing Falls Short

Traditional software testing assumes deterministic outputs. You write a test, you get a pass or fail. But AI systems don't work that way. They're probabilistic, and the outputs are often subjective.

Many teams use an exploratory approach: tweaking prompts and parameters until something looks good. But what works in a demo might fail in production. And without proper testing, you're flying blind.

"AI is a powerful tool. Without QA, it's like letting a helpful stranger fly your jet plane. It might work out, but it's not a smart risk to take."

Our Solution

AIQA brings data-science QA practices to generative AI. We provide:

  • Automatic tracing - Capture inputs, outputs, and performance without changing your workflow
  • Experiment management - Run systematic tests across different scenarios
  • OpenTelemetry integration - Built on industry standards for compatibility
  • Multi-language support - Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, and Go clients

Our Philosophy

We believe testing should be:

  • Simple - No need to become a testing expert. Just add a decorator and you're tracing.
  • Reliable - Catch issues before they become problems in production.
  • Built for AI - Understands probabilistic systems and subjective outputs.
