
# AIQA Brand Guidelines

Short reference for humans and AI when creating content or images for AIQA.

- **Name:** Use "AIQA" (all caps). Product: AI quality assurance and evaluation (traces, experiments, LLM-as-judge).
- **Mission:** Help people and companies make better, safer, and more reliable applied AI solutions.
- **Values:** Trust, ease of use
- **Tone:** Clear, trustworthy, playful, friendly, helpful. No hype or marketing fluff.
- **Primary Audience:** Software developers
- **Secondary Audiences:** Data-scientists, Dev-Ops, MLOps, AI professionals, software business people
- **Content:** Be accurate and concise. Prefer examples over long explanations. KISS. If using jargon, give a quick gloss.
- **Images:** Colourful, clean. Icons are clean yet colorful and have character.
